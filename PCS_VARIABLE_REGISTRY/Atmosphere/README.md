# Atmosphere

## 1. Scientific Scope

The Atmosphere domain covers observations of the gaseous envelope of Earth, including thermodynamic state, circulation, radiation, clouds, atmospheric composition, aerosols, and trace gases.

## 2. Major Subdomains

- Temperature
- Pressure
- Radiation
- Clouds
- Wind
- Atmospheric Chemistry
- Aerosols
- Trace Gases

## 3. Primary Scientific Agencies

- NASA
- NOAA
- Copernicus
- ESA
- JAXA
- WMO

## 4. Role inside PCS

Atmospheric variables provide the thermal, radiative, chemical, and dynamical context for the Planetary Common State. They support assessment of planetary-scale energy balance, circulation, and atmospheric composition.
