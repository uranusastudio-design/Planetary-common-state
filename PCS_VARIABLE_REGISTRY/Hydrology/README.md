# Hydrology

## 1. Scientific Scope

The Hydrology domain covers terrestrial water storage and movement, including precipitation, runoff, groundwater, soil moisture, river discharge, drought, and flood-relevant conditions.

## 2. Major Subdomains

- Precipitation
- Runoff
- Soil Moisture
- Groundwater
- River Discharge
- Drought
- Flood Conditions
- Water Storage

## 3. Primary Scientific Agencies

- NASA
- NOAA
- Copernicus
- ESA
- USGS
- WMO

## 4. Role inside PCS

Hydrological variables represent freshwater availability, water-cycle variability, drought and flood stress, and terrestrial coupling between atmosphere, land, biosphere, and human systems.
