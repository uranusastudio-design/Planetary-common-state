# Ocean

## 1. Scientific Scope

The Ocean domain covers observations of the global ocean, including temperature, salinity, circulation, sea level, biogeochemistry, heat content, and air-sea exchange.

## 2. Major Subdomains

- Sea Surface Temperature
- Ocean Heat Content
- Salinity
- Circulation
- Sea Level
- Ocean Biogeochemistry
- Air-Sea Fluxes
- Marine Carbon

## 3. Primary Scientific Agencies

- NASA
- NOAA
- Copernicus
- ESA
- JAXA
- WMO

## 4. Role inside PCS

Ocean variables contribute to PCS by representing planetary heat storage, mass redistribution, circulation state, chemical exchange, and long-timescale climate memory.
