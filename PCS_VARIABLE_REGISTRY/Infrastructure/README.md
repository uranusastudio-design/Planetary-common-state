# Infrastructure

## 1. Scientific Scope

The Infrastructure domain covers built systems that support human activity, including transportation, communications, power grids, water systems, buildings, and critical services.

## 2. Major Subdomains

- Transportation
- Communication Networks
- Power Grid
- Water Infrastructure
- Buildings
- Critical Services
- Urban Systems
- Infrastructure Exposure

## 3. Primary Scientific Agencies

- World Bank
- UN
- National Infrastructure Agencies
- National Statistical Agencies
- NASA
- ESA

## 4. Role inside PCS

Infrastructure variables describe the physical and operational systems through which societies interact with environmental constraints and maintain adaptive capacity.
