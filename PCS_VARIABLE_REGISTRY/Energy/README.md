# Energy

## 1. Scientific Scope

The Energy domain covers energy production, consumption, efficiency, storage, grid state, energy access, and energy-related emissions.

## 2. Major Subdomains

- Energy Production
- Energy Consumption
- Energy Efficiency
- Electricity Grid
- Renewable Energy
- Fossil Energy
- Energy Storage
- Energy Access

## 3. Primary Scientific Agencies

- IEA
- World Bank
- UN
- National Energy Agencies
- NOAA
- NASA

## 4. Role inside PCS

Energy variables represent the energetic basis of human activity, infrastructure operation, emissions drivers, and adaptive capacity within the Planetary Common State.
