# Cryosphere

## Scientific Scope

The Cryosphere catalog covers future variable groups describing frozen water systems on land and sea.

## Future Variable Groups

- Sea Ice
- Ice Sheets
- Glaciers
- Snow Cover
- Permafrost
- Frozen Freshwater

## Possible Observation Sources

- NASA
- NOAA
- NSIDC
- Copernicus
- ESA
- USGS
- WMO

## Planned Primary Connector

NSIDC becomes the primary Cryosphere connector for future sea-ice observations, including Arctic Sea Ice Extent, Antarctic Sea Ice Extent, and Sea Ice Area. No actual variable entries are implemented in this catalog yet.
