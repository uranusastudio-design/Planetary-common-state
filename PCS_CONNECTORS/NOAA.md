# NOAA

## Scientific Role

NOAA provides atmospheric, oceanic, climate, weather, greenhouse gas, and environmental monitoring datasets.

## Available Observation Categories

- Atmospheric CO2
- Weather
- Climate records
- Ocean observations
- Sea level
- Atmospheric chemistry
- Extreme weather

## Update Frequency

NOAA products range from real-time operational feeds to monthly and annual climate records.

## Future PCS Integration

NOAA is a candidate provider for chemical, atmospheric, ocean, hydrology, and extreme-weather observations in PCS. No API is implemented in this registry entry.
