# MODIS Connector Source

## Provider

Moderate Resolution Imaging Spectroradiometer dataset family, primarily distributed through NASA data systems.

## Typical Datasets

Vegetation indices, land surface, fire, snow, aerosols, and remote-sensing products.

## Typical Update Frequency

Daily to monthly, depending on product.

## Expected Variables

NDVI, EVI, land surface temperature, fire indicators, snow cover, and aerosol indicators.
