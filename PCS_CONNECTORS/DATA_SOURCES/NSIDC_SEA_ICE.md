# NSIDC Sea Ice

## Dataset Description

The NSIDC Sea Ice Index provides sea-ice observations for the Arctic and Antarctic, including sea-ice extent and related sea-ice indicators derived from passive microwave satellite records.

## Variables

- Arctic Sea Ice Extent
- Antarctic Sea Ice Extent
- Sea Ice Area

## Provider

NSIDC

## Temporal Coverage

Sea Ice Index records are intended for daily and monthly sea-ice monitoring. Exact coverage depends on the official NSIDC product version and hemisphere-specific data file.

## Quality

Quality metadata must remain tied to the official NSIDC product documentation and connector validation output. Missing or unavailable observations must remain missing.

## Future PCS Role

NSIDC Sea Ice is the primary planned Cryosphere connector for PCS sea-ice monitoring. Validated connector outputs may support future Cryosphere domain status after Engine integration.

## Current PCS Status

Connector implemented v1.0. Data are confirmed only after official NSIDC records are successfully parsed and validated.
