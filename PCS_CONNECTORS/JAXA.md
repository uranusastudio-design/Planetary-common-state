# JAXA

## Scientific Role

JAXA provides satellite observations relevant to precipitation, water cycle, climate, land, ocean, atmosphere, and space science.

## Available Observation Categories

- Precipitation
- Water cycle
- Ocean
- Atmosphere
- Land surface
- Space environment

## Update Frequency

JAXA product cadence depends on mission and product type, including near-real-time, daily, monthly, and research-grade releases.

## Future PCS Integration

JAXA is a candidate provider for hydrology, atmosphere, ocean, geosphere, and space-environment observations. No API is implemented in this registry entry.
