# World Bank

## Scientific Role

The World Bank provides socioeconomic, development, infrastructure, energy, population, and resilience indicators relevant to coupled human-Earth system analysis.

## Available Observation Categories

- Population
- Economy
- Energy
- Infrastructure
- Development indicators
- Adaptive capacity
- Food and water context

## Update Frequency

World Bank datasets vary by indicator and source, with annual and periodic updates common.

## Future PCS Integration

World Bank indicators are candidate sources for human-system, energy, infrastructure, and adaptive-capacity observations in PCS. No API is implemented in this registry entry.
