# NASA

## Scientific Role

NASA provides satellite, model, and in-situ Earth and space science observations relevant to climate, atmosphere, ocean, land, cryosphere, biosphere, hydrology, and space environment monitoring.

## Available Observation Categories

- Temperature
- Radiation
- Sea level
- Ice mass
- Vegetation
- Land cover
- Hydrology
- Atmospheric composition
- Space environment

## Update Frequency

NASA data products vary from near-real-time to monthly, annual, and reprocessed research records.

## Future PCS Integration

NASA datasets are candidate sources for thermal, structural, biosphere, hydrology, geosphere, and space-environment PCS observations. No API is implemented in this registry entry.
