# ESA

## Scientific Role

ESA provides Earth-observation satellite missions, climate data records, geophysical observations, and space-environment data relevant to PCS.

## Available Observation Categories

- Land surface
- Cryosphere
- Ocean
- Atmosphere
- Gravity
- Vegetation
- Space environment

## Update Frequency

ESA products vary across missions and data services, ranging from near-real-time to delayed reprocessed science products.

## Future PCS Integration

ESA is a candidate provider for geosphere, cryosphere, ocean, atmosphere, biosphere, and space-environment observations. No API is implemented in this registry entry.
