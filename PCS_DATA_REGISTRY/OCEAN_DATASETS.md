# Ocean Datasets

| Observation Category | Potential Dataset | Potential Providers | Expected Update Frequency | Potential Resolution | Future PCS Variables |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sea Surface Temperature | NOAA OISST, GHRSST, ERA5 SST | NOAA, NASA, Copernicus, ECMWF | Daily to monthly | Global gridded | Sea-surface thermal state |
| Ocean Profiles | Argo float profiles | Argo, NOAA, international Argo program | Near-real-time to delayed mode | Profile observations | Ocean temperature, salinity |
| Ocean Heat | NOAA Ocean Heat Content, Argo-based products | NOAA, Argo | Monthly to annual | Basin/global products | Ocean heat content |
| Sea Level | AVISO altimetry, NASA Sea Level Change, Copernicus sea level products | AVISO, NASA, Copernicus Marine Service | Daily to monthly | Along-track or gridded | Sea-level state |
| Currents | Copernicus Marine ocean currents, OSCAR | Copernicus Marine Service, NOAA, NASA | Daily to monthly | Global gridded | Surface circulation |
| Salinity | SMOS, Aquarius, Argo salinity | ESA, NASA, Argo | Weekly to monthly | Gridded or profile | Ocean salinity |
| Biogeochemistry | SOCAT, GLODAP, Copernicus Marine biogeochemistry | SOCAT, GLODAP, Copernicus Marine Service | Monthly to multi-year | Gridded or station/cruise | Marine carbon and chemistry |
| Waves | NOAA WaveWatch III, Copernicus wave products | NOAA, Copernicus Marine Service | Hourly to daily | Global/regional gridded | Wave state |
| Marine Ecosystems | Ocean color MODIS, VIIRS, Sentinel-3 OLCI | NASA, NOAA, ESA, Copernicus | Daily to monthly | Global gridded | Marine productivity proxy |
