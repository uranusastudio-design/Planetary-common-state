# Atmosphere Datasets

| Observation Category | Potential Dataset | Potential Providers | Expected Update Frequency | Potential Resolution | Future PCS Variables |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Temperature | NASA GISTEMP, NOAA GlobalTemp, ERA5 | NASA, NOAA, ECMWF, Copernicus Climate Data Store | Monthly to annual | Global gridded or global index | Thermal state, temperature anomaly |
| Reanalysis | ERA5, MERRA-2, NCEP/NCAR Reanalysis | ECMWF, NASA, NOAA | Hourly to monthly | Global gridded | Atmospheric state, dynamics |
| Radiation | CERES EBAF, CERES SYN1deg | NASA | Monthly | Global gridded | Radiative balance, TOA flux |
| Atmospheric CO2 | NOAA GML CO2, Mauna Loa CO2 | NOAA | Monthly to annual | Station and global products | Atmospheric CO2 |
| Trace Gases | OCO-2, OCO-3, TROPOMI, NOAA GML records | NASA, ESA, Copernicus, NOAA | Daily to monthly | Satellite swath, gridded, station | Trace gases |
| Aerosols | MODIS Aerosol, VIIRS Aerosol, MERRA-2 Aerosol | NASA, NOAA | Daily to monthly | Satellite or reanalysis gridded | Aerosol loading |
| Clouds | MODIS Cloud, CERES Cloud, ISCCP | NASA, NOAA, WMO-linked programs | Daily to monthly | Global gridded | Cloud state |
| Precipitation | GPM IMERG, GPCP | NASA, JAXA, NOAA | Half-hourly to monthly | Global gridded | Atmospheric water input |
| Weather Extremes | NOAA Storm Events, WMO climate extremes records | NOAA, WMO | Event-based to annual | Event records | Extreme weather indicators |
