# Biosphere Datasets

|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Vegetation Index | MODIS MOD13, VIIRS vegetation indices, Copernicus vegetation products | NASA, NOAA, Copernicus | 8-day to monthly | Global gridded | Vegetation state |
| Forest Cover | Hansen Global Forest Change, ESA CCI Land Cover | University of Maryland, NASA, ESA | Annual | Global gridded | Forest cover |
| Land Productivity | MODIS NPP/GPP, Copernicus productivity products | NASA, Copernicus | 8-day to annual | Global gridded | Ecosystem productivity |

| Ecosystem Extent | ESA WorldCover, Copernicus land products | ESA, Copernicus | Annual to periodic | Global gridded | Ecosystem extent |
| Wetlands | Global Surface Water, Global Lakes and Wetlands Database | JRC, WWF-linked datasets, Copernicus | Monthly to periodic | Global gridded/vector | Wetland extent |
| Wildfire | MODIS/VIIRS active fire, GFED | NASA, NOAA, GFED | Daily to monthly | Global gridded/fire detections | Fire activity |
| Carbon Cycle | FLUXNET, MODIS carbon products, OCO-2 related products | FLUXNET, NASA | Daily to annual | Tower/satellite/gridded | Carbon uptake proxy |
