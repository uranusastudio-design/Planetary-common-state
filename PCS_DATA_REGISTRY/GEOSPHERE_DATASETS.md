# Geosphere Datasets

| Observation Category | Potential Dataset | Potential Providers | Expected Update Frequency | Potential Resolution | Future PCS Variables |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Land Cover | ESA CCI Land Cover, Copernicus Global Land Cover, MODIS Land Cover | ESA, Copernicus, NASA | Annual to periodic | Global gridded | Land-cover state |
| Topography | SRTM, ASTER GDEM, Copernicus DEM | NASA, METI, ESA, Copernicus | Static/periodic | Global DEM | Topographic context |
| Geology | USGS geological maps and global geology products | USGS, national geological surveys | Periodic | Map/vector | Geological context |
| Earthquakes | USGS Earthquake Catalog | USGS | Near-real-time | Event records | Seismic activity |
| Volcanoes | Smithsonian Global Volcanism Program, USGS volcano hazards | Smithsonian, USGS | Event/weekly to periodic | Event/catalog | Volcanic activity |
| Soil | SoilGrids, Harmonized World Soil Database | ISRIC, FAO | Periodic | Global gridded | Soil context |
| Surface Change | Landsat, Sentinel-1, Sentinel-2 change products | USGS, NASA, ESA, Copernicus | Days to months | Satellite gridded | Surface-change indicators |
| Gravity | GRACE/GRACE-FO gravity fields | NASA, GFZ, CSR, JPL | Monthly | Global gridded | Mass redistribution context |
