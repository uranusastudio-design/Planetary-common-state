# Hydrology Datasets

| Observation Category | Potential Dataset | Potential Providers | Expected Update Frequency | Potential Resolution | Future PCS Variables |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Precipitation | GPM IMERG, CHIRPS, GPCC | NASA, JAXA, UCSB Climate Hazards Center, WMO-linked programs | Half-hourly to monthly | Global gridded | Precipitation |
| River Discharge | GRDC, USGS streamgage data | GRDC, USGS | Daily to monthly | Station records | River flow |
| Soil Moisture | SMAP, ESA CCI Soil Moisture | NASA, ESA | Daily to monthly | Global gridded | Soil moisture |
| Groundwater | GRACE/GRACE-FO terrestrial water storage, USGS groundwater records | NASA, USGS | Monthly to periodic | Gridded/station | Groundwater proxy |
| Water Storage | GRACE/GRACE-FO, GLDAS | NASA | Monthly | Global gridded | Terrestrial water storage |
| Surface Water | JRC Global Surface Water, SWOT products | JRC, NASA, CNES | Monthly to mission product updates | Global gridded/vector | Surface water extent |
| Drought | US Drought Monitor, SPEI Global Drought Monitor | NOAA, USDA, University of Nebraska, CSIC | Weekly to monthly | Regional/global | Drought condition |
| Flood | Dartmouth Flood Observatory, Copernicus Emergency Management Service | DFO, Copernicus | Event-based | Event/regional | Flood occurrence |
