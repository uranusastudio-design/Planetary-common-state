# Cryosphere Datasets

| Observation Category | Potential Dataset | Potential Providers | Expected Update Frequency | Potential Resolution | Future PCS Variables |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sea Ice Extent | NSIDC Sea Ice Index | NSIDC, NOAA, NASA | Daily to monthly | Polar gridded/index | Sea-ice extent |
| Sea Ice Concentration | AMSR-E/AMSR2, passive microwave sea ice products | NASA, JAXA, NSIDC | Daily | Polar gridded | Sea-ice concentration |
| Ice Sheet Mass | GRACE, GRACE-FO mascon products | NASA, GFZ, CSR, JPL | Monthly | Regional/gridded | Ice mass change |
| Ice Elevation | ICESat-2 ATL products | NASA | Campaign/orbit product updates | Track-based | Ice elevation change |
| Glaciers | Randolph Glacier Inventory, WGMS glacier records | RGI, WGMS, NSIDC | Periodic | Glacier outlines/records | Glacier state |
| Snow Cover | MODIS snow cover, IMS snow and ice products | NASA, NOAA | Daily to monthly | Global/hemispheric gridded | Snow cover |
| Permafrost | GTN-P permafrost records, ESA CCI Permafrost | GTN-P, ESA | Annual to periodic | Station/gridded | Permafrost condition |
| Frozen Freshwater | Lake ice records, satellite lake ice products | NOAA, NSIDC, Copernicus | Seasonal to annual | Lake records/gridded | Frozen freshwater status |
