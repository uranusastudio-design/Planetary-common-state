# Scientific Integrity

PCS is a scientific research platform, not an authority by itself.

## Rules

- No fabricated data.
- No unsupported claims.
- No claim of solving open mathematical problems.
- No medical or planetary claims without validation.
- Human review required.
- Source provenance required.
- Validation required before operational use.

## Open Problem Boundary

PCS does not claim to solve Hilbert Problems or Millennium Problems. Such topics may be used only as inspiration or analytical method families.
