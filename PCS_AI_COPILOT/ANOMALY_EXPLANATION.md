# Anomaly Explanation

The AI Copilot may help explain possible anomalies in PCS outputs or connector records, but it must not overclaim causality.

## Recommended Language

Anomaly explanations should use cautious phrases such as:

- observed deviation;
- possible contributing factors;
- requires further validation;
- data source confidence.

## Anomaly Scope

An anomaly may refer to an unusual value, abrupt change, stale update, unexpected null value, inconsistent unit, or connector-health warning.

## Causality Boundary

The Copilot should not claim that one variable caused another without validated scientific analysis. It may identify patterns that require human review.

## Review Guidance

Every anomaly explanation should make clear whether the issue concerns the data source, connector status, validation layer, or future PCS interpretation.
