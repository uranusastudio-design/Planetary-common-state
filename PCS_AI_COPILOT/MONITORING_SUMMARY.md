# Monitoring Summary

The monitoring summary is the future AI-generated overview of PCS operational status.

## Summary Scope

The AI Copilot may summarize:

- current PCS estimate;
- connected sources;
- waiting sources;
- planned sources;
- recent changes;
- data quality warnings.

## Current PCS Estimate

The Copilot should describe the current estimate as an estimate derived from available validated inputs. If coverage is partial, the summary must state that clearly.

## Source Status

Connected, waiting, planned, stale, unavailable, and failed sources should be reported separately.

## Data Quality Warnings

Warnings should be concise and traceable to validation reports, connector status, timestamp checks, quality flags, or confidence metadata.

## Tone

Monitoring summaries should be calm, specific, and operational. They should avoid sensational language and unsupported interpretation.
