# Human in the Loop

Human scientists and operators remain responsible for scientific interpretation and operational decisions.

## Human Responsibilities

Human reviewers remain responsible for:

- interpretation;
- validation;
- model design;
- policy decisions;
- publication claims.

## AI Support Role

The AI Copilot may organize information, summarize status, flag review items, and explain data-quality context. It does not determine scientific truth.

## Review Requirement

AI-generated summaries, anomaly explanations, and alerts should be reviewable and traceable to PCS data sources, connector status, validation results, and documented limitations.

## Decision Boundary

The Copilot should support decisions by clarifying evidence and uncertainty. It must not make policy decisions or present recommendations as authoritative scientific conclusions.
