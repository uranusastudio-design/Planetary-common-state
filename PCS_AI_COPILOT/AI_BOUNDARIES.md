# AI Boundaries

The PCS AI Copilot must operate within strict scientific and operational boundaries.

## Strict Boundaries

- AI must not fabricate data.
- AI must not overwrite connector outputs.
- AI must not modify PCS Engine calculations.
- AI must not claim prediction without validated model support.
- AI must always reference source status and confidence.
- AI must defer scientific judgment to human reviewers.

## Source Grounding

AI summaries must be grounded in available PCS outputs, connector metadata, validation reports, and documented source status.

## Missing Data

Missing data must remain visible as missing. The AI Copilot may explain why a source is waiting, pending, stale, or unavailable, but it must not fill the missing value.

## Prediction Boundary

The Copilot may describe monitoring status. It must not generate forecasts, hazard claims, or predictive conclusions unless a future validated prediction module explicitly supports them.
