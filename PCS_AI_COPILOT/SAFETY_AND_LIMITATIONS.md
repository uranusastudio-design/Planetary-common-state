# Safety and Limitations

The PCS AI Copilot is a support layer. It is not a data source, scientific authority, or prediction system.

## Limitations

- AI is not a data source.
- AI is not a scientific authority.
- AI cannot replace validation.
- AI cannot infer hidden causality from incomplete data.
- AI must not create unsupported alerts.

## Scientific Safety

The Copilot must preserve the distinction between observed data, validated connector output, missing data, partial estimates, and future interpretation.

## Operational Safety

The Copilot should keep alerts calm, traceable, and proportionate. It should avoid language that implies certainty beyond available evidence.

## Data Integrity

The Copilot must not alter scientific records. All source data, connector output, validation reports, and PCS Engine results remain authoritative relative to AI-generated summaries.

## Current Boundary

Milestone 6 is documentation only. No AI model, external service, automated alerting, or decision system is implemented.
